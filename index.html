"Starting Over: From Factory Floors to the Virtual World of Coding"

Hello, world!

Well, I might as well start this blog in the traditional manner that every new coder does, because, that's what I am now—a budding coder, a junior developer in the making. But let's back up a moment. You may be wondering, "Who is this person, and why should I care about his journey?" I'll be honest, I'm not anyone famous or particularly successful. In fact, I've worn many hats over the years, but success? That's something that's been elusive for me. My name's John, a 44-year-old factory worker, and I'm here to share my story—a story of resilience, reinvention, and redemption. In the past, I've dipped my toes into various industries. I've grappled with the ins and outs of Real Estate, ventured into the complex labyrinth of mortgage loan origination, and even aspired to be a home builder. Yet, despite my best efforts, each venture ended up in failure. The culmination of these failed endeavors led me down a path I never thought I would traverse: bankruptcy. A daunting word that has the power to send shivers down anyone's spine. But here I am, standing in the aftermath, still breathing, and still searching for that spark—my true calling.

So, you might be asking, "Why coding? And why now?"

In the face of adversity, I've learned that it's never too late to start anew. The digital world, one that's growing at an unprecedented rate, seemed as good a place as any to begin again. Coding, with its endless possibilities and the capacity to create and innovate, attracted me like a moth to a flame. Plus, the beauty of our modern world is that we have a treasure trove of knowledge at our fingertips. Online platforms offer a wealth of resources, from introductory courses to advanced programming, allowing anyone with an internet connection and a will to learn the chance to become a developer. And that's the path I've chosen for myself. So, here I am, a factory worker turned aspiring developer, ready to dive headfirst into the world of algorithms, data structures, and semicolons. But I'm not just here to learn; I'm here to share every step of my journey with you. You'll find no sugarcoating here, no pretense. There will be times of struggle and frustration, I'm sure. Moments when I'll question my sanity for choosing to decipher the language of machines. Yet, I believe there will also be victories, no matter how small, moments of breakthrough and understanding. And through it all, I'll remain transparent, documenting each high and low, each success and failure, right here on this blog.

This journey I'm embarking on isn't just about acquiring a new skill or starting a new career. It's about resilience, about picking up the pieces and creating something new and beautiful from them. It's about proving that failure is not a dead-end, but a detour leading to a different, perhaps even better, destination. I hope that you'll join me on this adventure, share in my triumphs and setbacks, and perhaps even find a spark of inspiration for your own journey. So, buckle up and get ready to explore the world of coding with me. Here's to new beginnings, to learning, and above all, to the courage to never give up.

Signing off,

Russell, the future developer
